Visit Salt Lake City

Enjoy the beauty of the mountains and all the fun activities you can do all year long.

Top three activities to do in Salt Lake City

Go snowboard or ski up a mountain

Big Cottonwood Canyon has lots of resorts to ski or snowboard.

Have a campfire up the canyon

Little Cottonwood is a great place to camp and roast smores.

Go to a basketball game

The Utah Jazz are a professional NBA team that play here in Salt Lake.
